Amuse-Bouche

Whipped Goat Cheese Crostini with Honey and Thyme

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices baguette, toasted
  • 4 oz goat cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp honey, plus more for drizzling
  • Fresh thyme leaves
  • Cracked black pepper

Preparation:

Whip goat cheese with heavy cream until light and fluffy. Spread onto toasted baguette slices. Drizzle with honey and garnish with fresh thyme leaves and cracked black pepper.

Appetizer

Seared Scallops with Citrus Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 8 large sea scallops, patted dry
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 orange, juice and zest
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest
  • 1 shallot, finely minced
  • Microgreens for garnish

Preparation:

Heat oil and butter in a pan until very hot. Sear scallops for 1-2 minutes each side until golden and just cooked through. Whisk citrus juices with minced shallot and slowly drizzle in olive oil to create vinaigrette. Plate scallops, drizzle with vinaigrette, and garnish with citrus zest and microgreens.

Soup/Salad

Roasted Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Nutmeg, sage, salt and pepper to taste
  • Toasted pumpkin seeds for garnish

Preparation:

Roast squash with olive oil, salt, and pepper at 400°F for 25 minutes. Sauté onions and garlic until translucent. Combine roasted squash, sautéed onions and garlic in a pot with vegetable broth. Simmer for 15 minutes, then blend until smooth. Return to pot, add cream, and season with nutmeg, sage, salt, and pepper. Garnish with toasted pumpkin seeds.

Main Course

Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb

Ingredients:

  • 1 rack of lamb (8 ribs), trimmed
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Roasted fingerling potatoes and asparagus for serving

Preparation:

Season lamb with salt and pepper. Sear in hot pan until browned on all sides. Brush with Dijon mustard. Mix bread crumbs, herbs, garlic, and olive oil, then press onto mustard-coated lamb. Roast at 375°F for 25-30 minutes for medium-rare. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing between ribs. Serve with roasted fingerling potatoes and asparagus.

Dessert

Dark Chocolate Soufflé

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter, plus more for ramekins
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar, plus more for ramekins
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving

Preparation:

Preheat oven to 375°F. Butter and sugar 4 ramekins. Melt chocolate and butter together, then cool slightly. Whisk in egg yolks and vanilla. In separate bowl, beat egg whites with salt until foamy, then gradually add sugar and beat to soft peaks. Gently fold egg whites into chocolate mixture in thirds. Fill ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes until risen but still slightly jiggly in center. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.
